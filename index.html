Seljan Nasibli

Seljan Nasibli

Soprano from Baku, Azerbaijan residing in London. Having completed her Vocal Studies and Masters Degree in Vocal Performance at the The Royal College of Music in 2014, she made her début in King’s Place Theatre at the Schönberg Festival singing ‘Donde estas hermano?’ by Luigi Nono and her début at London’s Barbican Hall as a soloist in a tribute to the Azerbaijani composer Niyazi.
